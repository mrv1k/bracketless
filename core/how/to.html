how-to Bracketless

Does
It collapses text between brackets.

Does not
It does not collapse links, code or emphasised text.
The reason being that they're likely to contain important information.
Basic functionality (available on load)
Left click text between brackets to collapse it.
(The farmers in the valley grew potatoes, peanuts, and bored.)
Hover over collapsed text to display it.
Options
Brackets matching limits: customize minimum and maximum amounts.
Defaults are 13 and 255 characters respectively. (below 13 and above 255 will be ignored)
Automation: enable autoload and autoplay.
Open options
Push the black button!
