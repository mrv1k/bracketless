Bracketless - how to

Thank you for installing Bracketless!


Basic (available upon extension load)
Left click to collapse text between brackets (The gills kneading quietly, and the pectorals).
Left click again to expand text between brackets (The farmers in the valley grew potatoes, peanuts, and bored.)
Options
Brackets matching: customize minimum and maximum limits. Defaults are 13 and 255 characters respectively (below 13 and above 255 characters will get ignored)
Automation: enable autoloading and autoplaying
